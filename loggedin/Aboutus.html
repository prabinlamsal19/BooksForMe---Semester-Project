Who We Are


BOOKSFORME is the world’s largest site for readers and book recommendations. Our mission is to help people find and share books they love. BOOKSFORME launched in MARCH 2020.
A Few Things You Can Do On BOOKSFORME

Track the books you're reading, have read, and want to read.
Check out your personalized book recommendations. Our recommendation engine analyzes 10 thousand data points to give suggestions tailored to your literary tastes.
Find out if a book is a good fit for you from our community’s reviews.



A Message From Our Co-Founder

When I was in second grade, I discovered the Hardy Boys series. Ever since, I've loved to read — both for fun and to improve my mind. And I'm always looking for the next great book.

One afternoon while I was scanning a friend's bookshelf for ideas, it struck me: when I want to know what books to read, I'd also make a bookshelf.

So I decided to build a website – a place where I could make bookshelves.The team Matrix created this website for our school project but it could much more and reach to the world.

BOOKSFORME is that site. It is a place where you can see what kind of books are more popular than other. You can create "bookshelves" to organize what you've read (or want to read).You can find your next favorite book. And on this journey with your friends you can explore new territory, gather information, and expand your mind.

Know more with less.
Sumit Khanal
Co-Founder
